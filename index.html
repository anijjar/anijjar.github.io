Welcome to my project scrapbook

My name is Amanpreet Nijjar and I am an undergraduate computer engineering student at the University of Victoria. My work interests is in development of autonomous vehicles and mixed-signal circuit board design. Alongside my studies, I practice developing PCBs by volunteering my time to AUVIC, a robotics club that builds AUVs competitively for RoboSub. Myself and others learn about underwater robotics development under the guidance of faculty advisor Dr. Brad Buckham, a mechanical engineering professor with a background in underwater dynamics and controls.

Some fun facts: I like to spend my free time reading and listening to some old-school rap music. I think dark licorice is underrated and coffee is best taken black. I drive a 2008 Honda Accord Coupe and probably will for the rest of my life ☻
