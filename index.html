What I Can Do.

    While working for the Autonomous Underwater Vehicle Interdiscplinary Club under Dr. Brad Buckham, I built a printed circuit board to convert messages from CAN to USB, similar to to an FTDI USB-serial converter.

  • Develop software

    To make it easier to download job postings from my university's job site, I built a web scrapper using Selenium and Python. You simply enter your login credentials and watch the magic happen.

  • Troubleshoot hardware

    The problem I faced was a display not working at all. I solved this issue by probing the terminals with a multi-meter and oscilloscope and found that little current was being drawn by the part. Looking at the layout in detail, LEDs were placed along the path, increasing the resistance. After removing the extra LEDs, the display was able to draw enough current to power-up correctly.
