Hardware

Underwater Locator Beacon

Sends acoustic signals underwater via a piezo electric transducer and is controlled via bluetooth (prior to being submerged in water).

USB to CAN Bridge

Connects USB devices with a two-wire CAN bus. messages can be viewed/sent using a terminal emulator, such as Tera Term.

Audio Effects

Modulates an audio signal to echo or pitch through button presses. The status of the signal is given yb the seven-segment LED. Fast Fourier Transform is used to modulate the signal.
