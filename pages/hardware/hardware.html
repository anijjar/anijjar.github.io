CAN Dongle

A small piece of hardware that connects enables USB device to communicate with a 2-wire CAN bus. Messages can be read/sent from the computer using a terminal emulator, such as Tera Term, to confirm if custom PCBs are communicating properly.

Audio Effects

Creates an echo/pitching effect through button presess. The pitch level and modes are displayed using the 7-segment LEDs. The audio signal is filtered and passed through a STM where the effect is applied using FFT, until it is sent out the speaker.

Core Concepts: Analog Signal Processing

Final Comments

Effects were working correctly. The board was tested using Lady Gaga-Paparazzi. The song was accompined by static, but the it slightly improved after improving the connection. When connecting peripherals, we should always use proper connectors.
